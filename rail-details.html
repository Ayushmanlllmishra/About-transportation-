Rail Transport

What is Rail Transport?

Rail transport involves the movement of goods and passengers on vehicles running on railways, including freight trains and passenger trains.

Why is Rail Transport Important?

Rail transport is crucial for industrial development, connecting cities over long distances, and reducing road congestion.

Advantages of Rail Transport

  • High capacity for carrying goods and passengers
  • Cost-effective for long distances
  • Environmentally friendly

Disadvantages of Rail Transport

  • Fixed routes and schedules
  • High infrastructure cost
  • Limited access to remote areas

Examples of Rail Transport Usage

Examples include high-speed bullet trains, metro systems, and freight trains transporting coal, oil, and goods.
