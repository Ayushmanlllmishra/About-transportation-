Road Transport

What is Road Transport?

Road transport involves the movement of goods and passengers via vehicles such as cars, buses, and trucks on paved and unpaved roads.

Why is Road Transport Important?

Road transport is essential for connecting rural and urban areas, facilitating daily commutes, and enabling last-mile delivery services for businesses.

Advantages of Road Transport

  • Door-to-door service
  • Flexibility in routes and timings
  • Lower cost for short distances
  • Accessibility in remote areas

Disadvantages of Road Transport

  • Traffic congestion
  • Limited for long-distance transport
  • Environmental pollution

Examples of Road Transport Usage

Examples include home deliveries by couriers, city buses for public transport, and personal vehicles for commuting.
