Water Transport

What is Water Transport?

Water transport is the movement of people or goods via boats, ships, or ferries over bodies of water such as oceans, rivers, and lakes.

Why is Water Transport Important?

Water transport is important for global trade, particularly for bulk goods like oil, coal, and grain, as well as for travel in coastal areas.

Advantages of Water Transport

  • Cost-effective for transporting bulk goods
  • Capable of carrying large quantities over long distances
  • Environmentally friendly compared to road and air transport

Disadvantages of Water Transport

  • Slow speed compared to air and road transport
  • Dependence on waterways, subject to weather conditions
  • High maintenance costs for vessels and ports

Examples of Water Transport Usage

Examples include cargo ships, cruise ships, ferries, and fishing vessels.
